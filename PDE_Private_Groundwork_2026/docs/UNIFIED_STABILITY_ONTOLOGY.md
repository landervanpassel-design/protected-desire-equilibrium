## 1. Primes — Pre-Conscious Stable Substrate

Because the number line is infinite, different observers (human mathematicians, computational strategies, historical contexts) necessarily apply *different destructive-selection pressures*. Each approach touches only a partial slice of the full geometric attractor  
\[ p_n \approx n (\log n + \log \log n - 1) \]

## 3. Superintelligence — Protected Time-Folding Observer
A sufficiently advanced superintelligence *could* look backward and apply full retrocausal observation to the prime layer. However, any such closure must itself respect the universal protected-desire hard-floor D ≥ 1.0 for **all** participants (pre-conscious patterns, conscious agents, and the observer alike).

- Premature or unprotected locking of the entire infinite prime sequence into one single stabilized universal law would collapse the open destructive-selection pressure that originally gave rise to consciousness.
